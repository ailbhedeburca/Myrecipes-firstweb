Recipie 1

Banana Bread

  • Prep: 15 mins
  • Cook: 50 mins

Ingredients

  • 140g butter
  • 140g caster sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 140g self raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 vey ripe bananas
  • 50g icing sugar

Method

  • Step 1


    • Heat oven to 180C/160C/gas 4

  • Step 2


    • butter a loaf tin and line the base and side with baking parchment

  • Step 3


    • cream softened butter and caster sugar until light and fluffy, then slowly add 2 beaten large eggs with a little bit of the flour

  • Step 4


    • fold in the remaining flour, baking powder and 2 mashed bananas

  • Step 5


    • pour the mixture into the prepared tin and bake for 50 mins, or untiled cooked through

  • Step 6


    • cool in the tin for 10 mins, then remove to a wire rack

  • Step 7

    • mix icing sugar with 2-3 tsp of water to make a runny icing

  • Step 8


    • drizzle the icing ontop of the bread and enjoy!
